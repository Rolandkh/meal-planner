## RECIPES


### OTHER MEAL RECIPES

#### Hummus Power Bowl (Sunday)

**Ingredients:**
- 150g cooked quinoa
- 100g chickpeas (canned, drained)
- 80g hummus
- 50g cherry tomatoes, halved
- 50g cucumber, diced
- 30g red onion, diced
- 20g kalamata olives, sliced
- 10ml olive oil
- 5ml lemon juice
- Fresh parsley for garnish

**Steps:**
1. Cook quinoa according to package directions and let cool slightly
2. In a bowl, layer the cooked quinoa as the base
3. Arrange chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and olives on top
4. Add a generous dollop of hummus in the center
5. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice
6. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve


#### Grilled Salmon with Roasted Vegetables (Sunday)

**Ingredients:**
- 140g salmon fillet
- 100g broccoli florets
- 80g zucchini, sliced
- 60g bell pepper, sliced
- 15ml olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 5ml lemon juice
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh dill for garnish

**Steps:**
1. Preheat oven to 200C (400F)
2. Toss broccoli, zucchini, and bell pepper with 10ml olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper
3. Spread vegetables on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes
4. Meanwhile, brush salmon with remaining olive oil and season with salt and pepper
5. Heat a non-stick pan over medium-high heat
6. Cook salmon skin-side down for 4-5 minutes, then flip and cook 3-4 minutes more
7. Drizzle lemon juice over salmon
8. Serve salmon with roasted vegetables and garnish with fresh dill


#### Lentil Soup (Monday)

**Ingredients:**
- 120g red lentils, dried
- 200ml vegetable broth
- 100ml water
- 60g carrot, diced
- 50g celery, diced
- 40g onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 10ml olive oil
- 5g cumin
- 3g turmeric
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh cilantro for garnish

**Steps:**
1. Heat olive oil in a pot over medium heat
2. Saute onion, carrot, and celery until softened, about 5 minutes
3. Add garlic, cumin, and turmeric, cook for 1 minute until fragrant
4. Add lentils, vegetable broth, and water
5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes until lentils are tender
6. Season with salt and pepper
7. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve


#### Baked Barramundi with Steamed Greens (Monday)

**Ingredients:**
- 135g barramundi fillet
- 120g broccolini
- 80g green beans
- 50g asparagus
- 15ml olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 5ml lemon juice
- Lemon zest
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh thyme

**Steps:**
1. Preheat oven to 180C (350F)
2. Place barramundi on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper
3. Brush fish with 10ml olive oil, season with salt, pepper, and fresh thyme
4. Bake for 12-15 minutes until fish flakes easily
5. Meanwhile, steam broccolini, green beans, and asparagus for 5-7 minutes
6. Toss steamed vegetables with remaining olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, and zest
7. Serve fish with steamed greens on the side


#### Buddha Bowl (Tuesday)

**Ingredients:**
- 140g cooked brown rice
- 80g edamame, shelled
- 70g red cabbage, shredded
- 60g carrot, julienned
- 50g cucumber, sliced
- 40g avocado, sliced
- 30g tahini
- 15ml soy sauce
- 10ml rice vinegar
- 5ml sesame oil
- Sesame seeds for garnish

**Steps:**
1. Cook brown rice according to package directions
2. Steam or blanch edamame until tender
3. Arrange rice in a bowl as the base
4. Top with edamame, red cabbage, carrot, cucumber, and avocado in sections
5. Mix tahini, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and sesame oil to make dressing
6. Drizzle dressing over the bowl
7. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve


#### Pan-Seared Tofu with Stir-Fried Vegetables (Tuesday)

**Ingredients:**
- 150g firm tofu, pressed and cubed
- 100g bok choy, chopped
- 70g snow peas
- 60g mushrooms, sliced
- 50g bell pepper, sliced
- 15ml sesame oil
- 15ml soy sauce
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 10g fresh ginger, grated
- 5ml rice vinegar
- Sesame seeds for garnish

**Steps:**
1. Press tofu to remove excess moisture, then cut into cubes
2. Heat 10ml sesame oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat
3. Add tofu and cook until golden on all sides, about 8-10 minutes, then set aside
4. In the same pan, add remaining sesame oil
5. Stir-fry garlic and ginger for 30 seconds
6. Add mushrooms and bell pepper, cook for 3 minutes
7. Add bok choy and snow peas, cook for 2-3 minutes more
8. Return tofu to pan, add soy sauce and rice vinegar, toss to combine
9. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve


#### Whole Grain Wrap (Wednesday)

**Ingredients:**
- 1 whole grain tortilla wrap (70g)
- 80g hummus
- 60g mixed greens
- 50g cucumber, sliced
- 50g tomato, sliced
- 40g carrot, grated
- 30g feta cheese, crumbled
- 20g red onion, sliced
- 10ml olive oil
- 5ml balsamic vinegar

**Steps:**
1. Lay the whole grain tortilla flat
2. Spread hummus evenly over the tortilla
3. Layer mixed greens, cucumber, tomato, carrot, and red onion
4. Sprinkle feta cheese on top
5. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar
6. Fold the sides in and roll tightly
7. Cut in half and serve


#### Grilled Ocean Trout with Roasted Brussels Sprouts (Wednesday)

**Ingredients:**
- 140g ocean trout fillet
- 150g brussels sprouts, halved
- 70g cherry tomatoes
- 15ml olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 5ml lemon juice
- Lemon zest
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh rosemary

**Steps:**
1. Preheat oven to 200C (400F)
2. Toss brussels sprouts and cherry tomatoes with 10ml olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper
3. Spread on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes until caramelized
4. Meanwhile, brush trout with remaining olive oil and season with salt, pepper, and rosemary
5. Heat a grill pan over medium-high heat
6. Cook trout skin-side down for 4-5 minutes, then flip and cook 3-4 minutes more
7. Drizzle with lemon juice and zest
8. Serve trout with roasted brussels sprouts and tomatoes


#### Chickpea Salad (Thursday)

**Ingredients:**
- 180g chickpeas (canned, drained)
- 100g cooked bulgur wheat
- 60g cucumber, diced
- 50g cherry tomatoes, halved
- 40g red onion, finely diced
- 30g feta cheese, crumbled
- 20g kalamata olives, sliced
- 15ml olive oil
- 10ml lemon juice
- 5g dried oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh mint for garnish

**Steps:**
1. Cook bulgur wheat according to package directions and let cool
2. In a large bowl, combine chickpeas, bulgur, cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, and olives
3. Whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, oregano, salt, and pepper
4. Pour dressing over salad and toss well
5. Top with crumbled feta cheese
6. Garnish with fresh mint and serve
