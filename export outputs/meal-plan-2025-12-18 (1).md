## RECIPES


### OTHER MEAL RECIPES

#### Sardine Detox Power Bowl (Sunday)

**Ingredients:**
- 100g sardines
- 100g mixed greens
- 50g avocado
- 30g chia seeds
- 15ml olive oil

**Steps:**
1. Layer greens in bowl
2. Top with sardines
3. Add sliced avocado
4. Sprinkle chia seeds
5. Drizzle olive oil


#### Tofu Stir-Fry (Sunday)

**Ingredients:**
- 150g firm tofu
- 200g asparagus
- 30ml olive oil
- 15ml tamari

**Steps:**
1. Press tofu to remove moisture
2. Cut tofu into cubes
3. Chop asparagus
4. Stir-fry tofu until golden
5. Add asparagus, cook 3 minutes
6. Drizzle with tamari


#### Mackerel Anti-Inflammatory Salad (Monday)

**Ingredients:**
- 100g mackerel
- 100g mixed greens
- 50g kimchi
- 30g walnuts
- 15ml olive oil

**Steps:**
1. Flake mackerel
2. Mix with greens
3. Top with kimchi
4. Add chopped walnuts
5. Drizzle olive oil


#### Baked Salmon with Cauliflower Rice (Monday)

**Ingredients:**
- 150g salmon
- 300g cauliflower
- 30ml olive oil
- Lemon zest

**Steps:**
1. Pulse cauliflower in food processor
2. Roast cauliflower rice
3. Bake salmon at 200°C for 12 minutes
4. Serve with lemon zest


#### Zucchini Noodle Protein Bowl (Tuesday)

**Ingredients:**
- 200g zucchini noodles
- 100g Greek yogurt
- 50g sardines
- 30g chia seeds

**Steps:**
1. Spiralize zucchini
2. Mix yogurt with chia seeds
3. Top with sardines
4. Chill before serving


#### Tofu and Vegetable Medley (Tuesday)

**Ingredients:**
- 150g firm tofu
- 200g mixed vegetables
- 30ml olive oil
- 15ml tamari

**Steps:**
1. Cube tofu
2. Chop mixed vegetables
3. Stir-fry tofu until crispy
4. Add vegetables
5. Season with tamari


#### Anti-Inflammatory Green Goddess Bowl (Wednesday)

**Ingredients:**
- 100g mixed greens
- 50g avocado
- 30g kimchi
- 50g Greek yogurt

**Steps:**
1. Layer greens
2. Top with avocado
3. Add kimchi
4. Drizzle yogurt dressing


#### Grilled Mackerel with Roasted Vegetables (Wednesday)

**Ingredients:**
- 150g mackerel
- 200g mixed vegetables
- 30ml olive oil
- Lemon

**Steps:**
1. Marinate mackerel in lemon
2. Roast mixed vegetables
3. Grill mackerel 4 minutes each side
4. Serve together


#### Fasting Preparation Protein Plate (Thursday)

**Ingredients:**
- 100g Greek yogurt
- 50g avocado
- 30g chia seeds
- 50g mixed nuts

**Steps:**
1. Mix yogurt with chia seeds
2. Top with avocado
3. Add mixed nuts
4. Eat slowly and mindfully


#### Gentle Break-Fast Bowl (Friday)

**Ingredients:**
- 100g Greek yogurt
- 50g mixed berries
- 30g chia seeds

**Steps:**
1. Gently mix yogurt
2. Add berries
3. Sprinkle chia seeds
4. Eat slowly


#### Light Sardine and Vegetable Plate (Friday)

**Ingredients:**
- 100g sardines
- 150g mixed vegetables
- 30ml olive oil

**Steps:**
1. Steam mixed vegetables
2. Gently warm sardines
3. Drizzle with olive oil
4. Serve with care


#### Energy Boosting Superfood Salad (Saturday)

**Ingredients:**
- 100g mixed greens
- 50g Greek yogurt
- 30g chia seeds
- 50g avocado
- 30g walnuts

**Steps:**
1. Chop greens
2. Mix yogurt with chia seeds
3. Top salad with avocado and walnuts
4. Drizzle with olive oil


#### Wild Salmon with Roasted Broccoli (Saturday)

**Ingredients:**
- 150g wild salmon
- 300g broccoli
- 30ml olive oil
- 50g kimchi

**Steps:**
1. Preheat oven to 200°C
2. Toss broccoli with olive oil
3. Roast broccoli for 20 minutes
4. Pan-sear salmon 4 minutes each side
5. Serve with kimchi
