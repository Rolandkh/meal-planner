## RECIPES

### PROTEIN BAR RECIPE
**Make on Saturday - yields 12 bars**

#### DRY INGREDIENTS:
- [ ] 200g rolled oats
- [ ] 100g walnuts, chopped
- [ ] 80g plant protein powder
- [ ] 30g cacao powder
- [ ] 15g maca powder
- [ ] 50g chia seeds
- [ ] 30g oat flour
- [ ] 100g dried blueberries
- [ ] 10g Lion's Mane powder
- [ ] 5g Reishi powder
- [ ] 5g turmeric powder
- [ ] 20g ground flaxseed

#### WET INGREDIENTS:
- [ ] 260g almond butter
- [ ] 100ml maple syrup
- [ ] 2 ripe bananas (200g), mashed
- [ ] 60ml plant milk
- [ ] 5g fresh ginger, grated

#### CHOCOLATE COATING:
- [ ] 200-250g dark chocolate
- [ ] 30-40g coconut oil

#### METHOD:
1. Line 20×20cm tin with parchment
2. Melt 100-125g chocolate with 15-20g coconut oil
3. Pour into tin, spread evenly, refrigerate 10 min
4. Mix all dry ingredients in large bowl
5. Warm 260g almond butter, 100ml maple syrup, and 60ml plant milk until smooth
6. Remove from heat, stir in 200g mashed bananas and 5g grated ginger
7. Pour wet into dry, mix until sticky
8. Spread over set chocolate, press VERY firmly
9. Refrigerate 2-3 hours
10. Melt remaining chocolate with remaining coconut oil
11. Pour over top, spread evenly, refrigerate 30 min
12. Cut into 12 bars (warm knife between cuts)
13. Wrap individually, store in fridge up to 2 weeks


### OTHER MEAL RECIPES

#### Sardine and Spinach Frittata (Sunday)

**Ingredients:**
- 2 eggs
- 100g sardines
- 100g spinach
- 15ml olive oil
- salt
- pepper

**Steps:**
1. Whisk eggs with salt and pepper
2. Sauté spinach in olive oil
3. Add sardines to spinach
4. Pour eggs over mixture
5. Cook on stovetop, then finish under grill


#### Tofu Stir-Fry with Cauliflower Rice (Sunday)

**Ingredients:**
- 200g firm tofu
- 300g cauliflower
- 50g baby carrots
- 15ml olive oil
- kimchi for garnish

**Steps:**
1. Rice cauliflower in food processor
2. Cube tofu and pat dry
3. Stir-fry tofu in olive oil
4. Add carrots and cauliflower rice
5. Cook until vegetables are tender
6. Serve with kimchi


#### Avocado and Mackerel Salad (Monday)

**Ingredients:**
- 100g mackerel
- 1 avocado
- 100g mixed greens
- 30g sauerkraut
- 15ml olive oil

**Steps:**
1. Slice avocado
2. Mix greens and mackerel
3. Top with avocado
4. Add sauerkraut
5. Drizzle with olive oil


#### Pan-Seared Wild Salmon with Sautéed Spinach (Monday)

**Ingredients:**
- 150g wild salmon
- 200g spinach
- 15ml olive oil
- lemon
- salt
- pepper

**Steps:**
1. Season salmon with salt and pepper
2. Sear salmon in hot pan, 4 minutes each side
3. Sauté spinach in olive oil
4. Serve salmon over spinach
5. Garnish with lemon


#### Greek Yogurt Power Bowl (Tuesday)

#### Baked Tofu with Roasted Broccoli (Tuesday)

- 200g firm tofu
- 300g broccoli
- 15ml olive oil
