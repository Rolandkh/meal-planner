### CENTER AISLES

#### AISLE 1 - HEALTH FOODS & GRAINS MEGA-AISLE
**Length:** Longest aisle in store  
**Primary categories:** Grains, health foods, wraps, nut butters, seeds

**Products:**
- **Packaged bread:** Abbot's, Bergen, Tip Top (in bags/plastic)
- **Crumpets** (6-packs, 10-packs)
- **Whole grains:** Quinoa, bulgur, farro
- **Seeds:** Chia seeds, flaxseed (ground or whole), sunflower seeds
- **Wraps:** Whole grain wraps, tortillas
- **Nut butters:** Almond butter, peanut butter, tahini
- **Health products:** Plant-based protein powder, cacao powder, nutritional yeast, maca powder
- **Spreads:** Vegemite
- **Other:** Oat flour

**Meal plan items from Aisle 1:**
- Crumpets (Maia's breakfast)
- Quinoa (Roland's buddha bowls)

## OPTIMAL SHOPPING ROUTE

For the **Diet Compass Meal Plan**, follow this route for maximum efficiency:

### Route Order:
1. **Fresh Produce** (enter store)
   - Salad greens, vegetables, fruits
   - Fresh herbs, ginger
   - Bulk walnuts (if available)

2. **Aisle 1** (LONGEST - allow extra time)
   - Crumpets, quinoa, wraps
   - Chia seeds, flaxseed, sunflower seeds
   - Almond butter, tahini
   - Protein powder, cacao powder, nutritional yeast

3. **Aisle 2**
   - Rolled oats (bulk purchase for protein bars)
   - Plant milk
   - Herbal tea

4. **Aisle 3**
   - Dark chocolate (200-250g for protein bars)
   - Maple syrup
   - Walnuts (packaged), dried blueberries
   - Whole grain crackers

5. **SKIP Aisle 4** (snacks not needed)

6. **Aisle 5**
   - Canned chickpeas, lentils
   - Pasta (for Maia)
   - Tamari, kimchi, sauerkraut
   - Turmeric powder
   - Ready-made soups (lentil, vegetable)

7. **Aisle 6**
   - Canned sardines, tuna, mackerel (in olive oil)
   - Olive oil (large bottle)
   - Coconut oil

8. **Dairy Section**
   - Greek yogurt (1kg tubs)
   - Kefir
   - Firm tofu, smoked tofu
   - Hummus (multiple tubs)

9. **Meat & Seafood**
   - Fresh salmon (150g portions)
   - Other fresh fish as needed

10. **Frozen Section**
    - Frozen stir-fry vegetables
    - Frozen berries

11. **Bread & Bakery** (if fresh bread needed)

## STORE-SPECIFIC TIPS

### Coles Caulfield Village
- **Busiest times:** Saturday mornings, weekday 5-7pm
- **Quietest times:** Weekday mornings (9-11am), Sunday evenings
- **Fresh produce:** Restocked early morning (best quality 8-10am)
- **Markdown times:** Late evening (after 8pm) for near-expiry items
- **Parking:** Underground parking available
- **Trolleys:** Available near entrance
- **Self-checkout:** Available for small shops
- **Click & Collect:** Available (order online, pick up in store)

### Budget Tips
- **Coles brand:** Use for basics (yogurt, canned goods, oats) - often 20-30% cheaper
- **Bulk buying:** Oats, seeds, chocolate (when on special) - store extras
- **Specials:** Check catalog for protein powder, nuts, fish (buy extra and freeze)
- **Avoid:** Pre-cut vegetables (pay premium), individual portion packs
