# La Dieta

## Profile Overview

**Profile Name:** La Dieta  
**Profile Type:** Temporary / Event-Based  
**Duration:** Minimum 7 days, can start weeks or months before retreat (requires retreat date)  
**Dietary Category:** Cleansing & Preparation

## Intention & Goals

### Primary Objectives
- **Physical Cleansing:** Remove stimulants and toxins to calm and cleanse the body
- **Energetic Preparation:** Create optimal conditions for deep healing and spiritual connection
- **Safety:** Eliminate fermented foods and certain substances that could create adverse interactions
- **Digestive Rest:** Progressively lighten food intake to allow the body to relax and prepare

### Key Principles
- The lighter the food, the easier access to deep healing
- Start as early as possible, but minimum 7 days before retreat
- Progressive restriction increases closer to retreat date
- Focus on clean, fresh, unprocessed foods

---

## Timeline Structure

This diet plan operates on a reverse timeline from the retreat date. When creating meal plans, calculate backwards from the retreat date:

- **Phase 1 (At least 7 days before, can start earlier):** Base cleansing phase - can begin weeks or even months before retreat
- **Phase 2 (Final 3 days before retreat):** Enhanced restriction phase  
- **Phase 3 (Day of retreat):** Minimal food phase

**Important:** The diet MUST begin at least 7 days before the retreat, but participants are encouraged to start earlier. The earlier you begin, the more thorough the cleansing process.

## Phase 2: Final 3 Days Before Retreat
### Enhanced Restriction Phase

**All restrictions from Phase 1 continue, PLUS the following additional restrictions:**

#### ADDITIONAL FOODS TO ELIMINATE

**Fermented Products:**
- All soy products
- Sauerkraut
- All vinegars
- Pickles
- Kombucha
- Miso
- Yoghurt (previously allowed, now restricted)

**Other Restrictions:**
- All salt (even land-based salt)
- Ripe avocado (especially with mushy brown bits)
- Ripe banana (especially with brown bits)
- Coconut & coconut products (including coconut water)
- Figs

#### FOODS STILL ALLOWED
- All "Yes" foods from Phase 1 EXCEPT those listed above
- Continue emphasis on fresh vegetables, fresh fruit, clean proteins, simple grains

## Phase 3: Day of Retreat
### Minimal Food Phase

#### ALLOWED FOODS
**Light, easily digested foods only:**
- Soups (light, clear broths)
- Eggs
- Fish
- Chicken  
- Fruit smoothies

#### MEAL TIMING STRUCTURE
- **Morning:** Good breakfast (from allowed foods)
- **~1pm:** Light lunch (from allowed foods)
- **1pm-2pm:** Only fresh fruit
- **After 2pm:** ONLY water, lemon juice, and light herbal teas
- **No solid food after 2pm**

## Additional Restrictions & Considerations

### Medications (4 weeks prior minimum)
**Must be stopped (with medical supervision):**
- Antidepressants (SSRIs)
- Sleep medications
- Barbiturates
- Alpha- and beta-blockers
- Steroids
- Antibiotics (1 week minimum)
- Codeine products

**Only permitted pain medication:** Advil

### Substances to Avoid (4 weeks before and after)
- Psychedelics (LSD, psilocybin)
- Street drugs (ice, cocaine, amphetamines, ketamine, opiates, MDMA)
- Marijuana (1 week prior minimum)

### Lifestyle Considerations (3 days before and after)
- Sexual abstinence (including masturbation) to conserve energy
- Avoid violent or disturbing media content (especially 3 days prior)

## Meal Planning Guidelines for Vanessa

### When Creating Meal Plans:

1. **Calculate Timeline & Ask About Start Date:** 
   - Always ask for the retreat date
   - Ask when the user wants to begin the diet (or if they want to start immediately)
   - Calculate which phase the user is in based on their current position relative to the retreat date
   - Minimum requirement: Must start at least 7 days before retreat
   - Recommended: Starting 2-4 weeks before allows more thorough cleansing

2. **Progressive Simplification:** 
   - Phase 1 meals can be more varied and substantial (but still within restrictions)
   - Phase 2 (final 3 days) should emphasize lighter, simpler preparations
   - Phase 3 (day of) requires strict timing and minimal food intake
   - The longer someone is in Phase 1, the lighter they may want to go as they approach Phase 2

3. **Hydration Emphasis:** 
   - Include lemon water with every meal plan
   - Suggest herbal teas throughout the day
   - Increase hydration recommendations closer to retreat date

4. **Meal Structure Suggestions:**
   - Phase 1 (7+ days before): Three regular meals, can include all Phase 1 foods. Duration flexible - can be weeks or months
   - Phase 2 (final 3 days): Three lighter meals, enhanced restrictions apply
   - Phase 3 (day of): Structured timing is critical - breakfast, light lunch at 1pm, fruit until 2pm, then liquids only

5. **Recipe Considerations:**
   - Favor simple cooking methods (steaming, light saut√©ing, baking)
   - Avoid complex sauces or heavy preparations
   - Emphasize natural flavors
   - Include herbs for flavor (since strong spices are restricted)

6. **Shopping List Priorities:**
   - Fresh produce (vegetables and fruits)
   - Quality proteins (free-range chicken, fresh fish)
   - Simple grains
   - Herbal teas and lemons
   - Olive oil

### Important Reminders When Planning:

- Always verify which phase the user is in based on retreat date
- Ask when they want to start the diet if not already begun (can start anytime, minimum 7 days before)
- Encourage starting earlier than the minimum 7 days for more thorough cleansing
- Emphasize the importance of the timing restrictions on Phase 3 (day of retreat)
- Remind users that starting the diet earlier allows for deeper preparation
- Note that this is a temporary cleansing diet, not a long-term eating plan
- Suggest users consult with retreat organizers if they have specific questions
- Remind users to communicate any medications or health conditions to retreat organizers

## Notes for Implementation

- Retreat date (essential for timeline calculation)
- Desired diet start date or confirmation to start immediately
- Current date (to determine which phase and how many days in Phase 1)
- Any medications or health conditions (to flag potential issues)

**Vanessa Should:**
- Ask when the user wants to begin if not starting immediately
- Automatically calculate which phase based on dates
- Provide appropriate meal suggestions for current phase
- Accommodate extended Phase 1 periods (weeks or months)
- Warn when transitioning between phases (especially into Phase 2 and Phase 3)
- Give reminders about Phase 3 meal timing
- Offer substitutions within allowed foods
- Create shopping lists that match the current phase restrictions

**Recipe Formatting:**
- All ingredients in grams (per user preference)
- Include ingredient quantities in instructions as well as ingredients list
- Keep recipes simple and aligned with cleansing principles
